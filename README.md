# 📊 E-Commerce Business Insights & Customer Behavior Analysis (SQL)

## 🧾 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an e-commerce dataset to uncover insights related to **business performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends** using SQL.

The goal is to simulate real-world business analysis by solving multiple business problems and generating actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

In a competitive e-commerce environment, understanding customer behavior and revenue drivers is crucial for growth.

This project analyzes transactional data to:

* Evaluate business performance
* Understand customer purchasing patterns
* Identify high-value customers
* Improve retention and revenue strategies

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Source: Olist Brazilian E-commerce Dataset
* Contains multiple tables:

  * customers
  * orders
  * order_items
  * products
  * sellers
  * order_payments
  * order_reviews
  * product_category_name_translation

## 📌 Key Business Questions Solved

### 🔹 Business Performance

* Total number of orders
* Total revenue generated
* Average order value (AOV)
* Orders trend (daily/monthly)

### 🔹 Customer Analysis

* New vs Returning Customers
* Repeat Purchase Rate
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Top 10 Customers by Revenue

### 🔹 Product Analysis

* Top-selling products
* Revenue by category
* Least performing products

### 🔹 Sales Analysis

* Revenue trends over time
* Peak sales periods
* Revenue by location

### 🔹 Order & Delivery Analysis

* Average delivery time
* Late delivery percentage
* Order status distribution

### 🔹 Payment Analysis

* Most used payment methods
* Payment value distribution

### 🔹 Advanced Analysis

* Cohort Analysis
* RFM Segmentation
* Running totals & moving averages

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 📌 Majority of customers are **one-time buyers (~97%)**, indicating low retention
* 📌 Strong **growth trend observed from 2017 to 2018**
* 📌 Peak sales during **festive seasons (Nov–Dec)**
* 📌 A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue
* 📌 Average Order Value indicates **moderate spending behavior**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Improve customer retention using loyalty programs
* Increase AOV through bundling and upselling
* Target high-value customers with personalized offers
* Optimize delivery process to enhance customer experience
